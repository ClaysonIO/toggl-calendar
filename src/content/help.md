# Help

## General Questions

### How can I see how many hours I need to work this week to meet my annual targets?

Go to the **Year** view and set your fiscal year and annual target (hours or percentage of full-time). The header shows hours remaining and a suggested daily and weekly target based on the number of working days left in the year. You can also use the **Week** view to set weekly plans and see how they compare to your targets.

### How do I set or change my annual target?

On the **Year** view, use the edit-target control to set your target as either total hours for the year or a percentage of full-time hours. The app then shows your progress and how many hours per day or week you need to stay on track.

### How do I set when my fiscal year starts?

On the **Year** view, click **Fiscal year start** and choose the first month of your fiscal year. The year view and all targets use this range.

### What can I do on the Week view?

The **Week** view shows your time by project for the selected week. You can see projected vs actual hours, set a weekly plan and billable target per project, and switch between rounded and actual time. In Toggl mode you also get task descriptions and project notes (markdown). In Manual mode you enter time per project for each day.

### What can I do on the Year view?

The **Year** view shows a calendar of months for your fiscal year. You can toggle between **Projected** (planned hours per day) and **Actual** (logged time), filter by **Billable**, **Non-billable**, or **All**, and filter by project. Click a day to edit its projected hours. In Toggl mode use **Sync year** to pull the latest data; in Manual mode you enter or edit time when you click a day.

### What does the Projects view show?

The **Projects** view lists companies and projects for the selected fiscal year, with total hours per project. In Toggl mode it uses your chosen workspace; in Manual mode it shows the companies and projects you have added. Use the year selector to change which fiscal year you are viewing.

## Toggl

### How do I sync my time from Toggl?

Open the **Year** view and click **Sync year**. The app fetches time entries from the Toggl API for the current fiscal year and stores them locally (in your browser). Data is also synced when you use the Week view. Make sure your API key and workspace are set in **Config** (gear button in the header).

### How do I configure my Toggl API key and workspace?

Click **Config** (gear icon) in the header when in Toggl mode. Enter your Toggl API key and select the workspace you want to use. Your selection is remembered for next time.

## Manual

### How do I switch to manual entry (no Toggl)?

Click **Manual** in the header next to **Toggl**. The app then uses only data you enter locally—no API key or sync. You can switch back to **Toggl** anytime.

### How do I add companies and projects in Manual mode?

In Manual mode, go to the **Projects** view (or the place where you manage manual projects). From there you can add companies and projects. These are stored in your browser only and are used for the Week and Year views when in Manual mode.

### How do I log time without using Toggl?

In Manual mode, use the **Week** view to enter time per project for each day. On the **Year** view, you can click a day to open the edit dialog and enter or adjust hours for that day. All data is stored locally.

### Can I use annual targets in Manual mode?

Yes. In Manual mode, the **Year** view works the same way: set your fiscal year start and annual target (hours or percentage). The app shows hours remaining and the suggested daily and weekly target based on your manually entered time.
